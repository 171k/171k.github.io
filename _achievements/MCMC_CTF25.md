# 5th Place: Intervarsity Cyber Forensics 2025

## Result

My team achieved 5th place in the Intervarsity Cyber Forensics 2025 competition.

The competition required participants to solve practical cybersecurity and digital-forensics challenges within a limited amount of time.

## My contribution

I contributed to the technical investigation and challenge-solving process, particularly in areas related to:

- Digital forensics
- File analysis
- Network traffic investigation
- Reverse engineering
- CTF problem-solving
- Team coordination

I helped analyse challenge artifacts, identify useful evidence, test possible solutions, and share findings with my teammates.

## Why it matters

This achievement demonstrated my ability to apply cybersecurity knowledge in a competitive and time-sensitive environment.

Finishing in the top five also reflected the importance of teamwork, communication, persistence, and structured investigation.

The experience further strengthened my interest in digital forensics, reverse engineering, and practical cybersecurity work.

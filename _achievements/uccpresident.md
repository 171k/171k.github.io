# President of UiTM Cyberheroes Club 2025/2026

## Result

I was appointed President of UiTM Cyberheroes Club for the 2025/2026 session.

UiTM Cyberheroes Club is a student organization focused on developing practical cybersecurity skills and strengthening the cybersecurity community among UiTM students.

## My contribution

As President, I am responsible for leading the club’s executive committee and coordinating its technical and organizational activities.

My responsibilities include:

- Planning cybersecurity programmes and competitions
- Coordinating committee members and technical teams
- Managing collaboration with lecturers, mentors, and external organizations
- Supporting cybersecurity workshops and training sessions
- Overseeing participant communication and event announcements
- Supporting sponsorship and partnership discussions
- Guiding challenge creators and infrastructure teams
- Representing the club in university and external activities

I also remain directly involved in technical work, including CTF platform development, challenge creation, infrastructure monitoring, and participant support.

## Why it matters

This role represents an important leadership milestone in my university experience.

It has taught me how to balance technical responsibilities with planning, communication, team management, and decision-making.

Leading the club has also allowed me to help other students gain practical exposure to cybersecurity through workshops, competitions, and collaborative projects.

# ACS EDU Digital Forensics Certificate of Completion

This certificate records my completion of digital forensics training through ACS EDU. The programme strengthened the structured and evidence-focused approach needed when investigating activity on digital systems.

## What it covered

- The role of digital forensics in cybersecurity investigations
- Careful handling and preservation of digital evidence
- Examining artifacts and building a defensible investigative timeline
- Recording observations and separating evidence from assumptions
- Presenting technical findings in a clear and repeatable way

## What I gained

The training reinforced the importance of patience, documentation, and evidence integrity. It helped me approach forensic artifacts more systematically and communicate findings in a way that another analyst can understand and reproduce.
